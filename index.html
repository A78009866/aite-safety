Child Safety Standards & CSAM Policy

App Name: Aite

Last Updated: February 2026

At Aite, we are committed to providing a safe environment for all users. We have a Zero-Tolerance Policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and the exploitation of children. Protecting minors is our top priority.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

We strictly prohibit the upload, sharing, distribution, or solicitation of content that depicts, promotes, or normalizes child sexual abuse or exploitation. This includes, but is not limited to, photos, videos, and text messages.

2. Detection and Monitoring

To ensure the safety of our platform, Aite employs the following measures:

  • User Reporting: We provide an easy-to-use in-app reporting mechanism allowing users to flag suspicious content or behavior immediately.
  • Content Moderation: We utilize moderation tools and human review processes to identify and remove illegal content violating our child safety standards.

3. Enforcement Actions

If any user is found violating this policy, we will take immediate action:

  • The offending content will be permanently removed.
  • The user's account will be immediately and permanently banned from accessing Aite.
  • We will preserve the data as required by law to assist in investigations.

4. Reporting to Authorities

In compliance with international and local laws, Aite will report any suspected cases of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) to the appropriate authorities, including:

  • The National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) via the CyberTipline.
  • Local law enforcement agencies where applicable.
